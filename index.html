Timber Privacy Policy

How we handle your data in the Timber timer app

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Timer & Block Data: Timer configurations, blocks, media references, and session history you create within the App. This data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Audio Recordings: If you use the audio recording feature, recorded audio is stored locally on your device in the app's designated storage directory.
  • Media Files: Audio and video files you import for use with timers are stored locally in the Documents/Timber folder on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device and hardware information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • General location data (derived from IP address)

Google AdMob uses this data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences. For more information, see the Google Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifiers (app user ID)
  • Purchase and subscription transaction data
  • Device type and OS version
  • Country/region (derived from App Store / Play Store locale)

RevenueCat does not collect personally identifiable information. For more details, see the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

Firebase Analytics (by Google) may collect:

  • App usage data (screen views, session duration, feature usage)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, screen resolution)
  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • General location data (country/region from IP address)

This data is used solely to understand how users interact with the App and to improve the user experience. See the Firebase Privacy Information.

Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

Firebase Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device state at the time of the crash (memory usage, disk space, battery state)
  • Device information (model, OS version, orientation)
  • Anonymous Crashlytics Installation UUID

This data is used exclusively to identify, diagnose, and fix app crashes and stability issues. See the Firebase Privacy Information.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not collect or transmit audio recordings, timer data, or any user-created content to external servers. We do not use any form of user authentication or account system.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All user-created content (timers, blocks, history, media files, audio recordings) is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite database and the device file system. We do not have access to this data.
  • Third-Party Data: Data collected by AdMob, RevenueCat, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics is stored on their respective servers. Please refer to their privacy policies for information about their data retention practices.
  • Security: We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the information handled by the App, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (toggle off)
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You may opt out of analytics collection through your device settings.
  • Delete Your Data: You can delete all locally stored data at any time through Settings → Clear History or Settings → Reset All Data within the App.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data, including the SQLite database. Media files stored in the Documents/Timber folder may persist and can be manually deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.
